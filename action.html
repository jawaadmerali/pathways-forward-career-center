Take Action

Practical next steps from exploration to decision

Junior & Senior Timeline

Junior Year, Fall

Take career assessments (personality, values, interests). Meet your counselor. Research 3-5 career clusters.

Junior Year, Spring

Job shadow or interview someone in your field. Take the PSAT/SAT or ACT. Build your resume. Find summer opportunities.

Senior Year, Fall

Finalize your plan. Apply to colleges or programs. Submit FAFSA (opens October 1). Apply for scholarships.

Senior Year, Spring

Compare financial aid offers. Decide by May 1. Complete enrollment or training registration.

Financial Basics

  • FAFSA: Required for federal aid. Free at StudentAid.gov. Opens October 1.
  • Grants vs. loans: Accept grants and scholarships first. Borrow only what you need.
  • Net price: Use the College Scorecard to estimate real cost after aid.
  • Scholarships: Apply early. Check local awards through your counselor.

Gain Experience

  • Ask your counselor about internships
  • Volunteer in fields that interest you
  • Search VolunteerMatch.org
  • Part-time jobs build skills and habits
