Career Development Framework

Social Cognitive Career Theory (SCCT) guides this site

Career choices are shaped by what you believe about yourself, what you expect from a path, and the supports or barriers around you (Lent, Brown, & Hackett, 1994, 2000). This site is built on that research.

SCCT Model

Based on Lent, Brown, and Hackett (1994, 2000).

Self-Efficacy

Your belief that you can succeed. It grows through trying, watching others succeed, encouragement, and practice (Bandura, 1997).

Outcome Expectations

What you expect from a career: pay, stability, respect. Our Explore section helps you get accurate information.

Supports & Barriers

Family, counselors, mentors, and finances all matter. See Equity and Community for support.
